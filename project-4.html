Healthcare Platform

Complete redesign of a healthcare platform to improve patient experience, streamline doctor workflows, and ensure accessibility compliance.

Role

Lead Designer

Duration

8 months

Team

15 members

Platform

Web & Mobile

The Challenge

MedConnect's existing platform was difficult to navigate, not accessible to users with disabilities, and created friction for both patients and healthcare providers. The system needed a complete overhaul to meet modern standards and improve healthcare outcomes.

34% Task Completion Rate
12min Avg. Appointment Booking
0% Accessibility Compliance

Research & Understanding

Conducted extensive research with patients, doctors, and healthcare staff to understand the complex ecosystem and requirements.

Stakeholder Interviews

Interviewed 30+ stakeholders including patients, doctors, nurses, and administrators to understand different needs.

Clinical Observations

Shadowed healthcare providers during their daily workflows to identify pain points and inefficiencies.

Accessibility Audit

Conducted comprehensive WCAG 2.1 audit and tested with users who have disabilities.

Regulatory Research

Studied HIPAA requirements and healthcare regulations to ensure compliance in the design.

Critical Insights

  • Patients often feel anxious and need clear, reassuring communication
  • Healthcare providers work under extreme time pressure and need efficient workflows
  • Accessibility isn't just compliance - it directly impacts health outcomes
  • Trust and privacy are paramount in healthcare interactions
  • Mobile usage is high among younger patients, but many older patients prefer web

Design Solution

Created a platform that prioritizes clarity, accessibility, and efficiency while maintaining the highest standards of healthcare privacy and security.

Patient-Centered Design

Clear information architecture and gentle UI that reduces anxiety and builds confidence.

Provider Efficiency

Streamlined workflows that save time while ensuring comprehensive patient care.

Universal Accessibility

WCAG 2.1 AA compliant design that works for users of all abilities and assistive technologies.

Secure Communication

HIPAA-compliant messaging system that enables safe patient-provider communication.

Key Features

Patient Portal

Intuitive interface for patients to manage appointments, view test results, and communicate with their care team.

Provider Dashboard

Comprehensive view of patient information, schedules, and clinical tools designed for quick access and decision-making.

Appointment System

Simplified booking process with real-time availability, automated reminders, and easy rescheduling options.

The redesigned platform significantly improved both patient satisfaction and healthcare provider efficiency:

Healthcare Impact

  • Reduced no-show appointments by 42% through better communication and reminders
  • Increased patient engagement with care plans by 56%
  • Saved healthcare providers an average of 15 minutes per patient interaction
  • Improved medication adherence tracking and patient outcomes
  • Successfully passed all healthcare compliance audits
"The new platform has transformed how we interact with our patients. Everything is clearer, faster, and more secure. Both our staff and patients love the improved experience." - Dr. Jennifer Martinez, Chief Medical Officer

Design Principles & Learnings

  • Empathy is critical in healthcare: Understanding the emotional state of users in medical contexts informed every design decision.
  • Accessibility benefits everyone: Designing for disabilities created a clearer, more usable experience for all users.
  • Regulatory constraints can inspire creativity: HIPAA requirements led to innovative solutions for secure communication.
  • Progressive enhancement works: Building core functionality first ensured the platform worked across all devices and connections.
  • Multi-stakeholder design is complex: Balancing needs of patients, providers, and administrators required careful prioritization.
