Online Learning Platform

Complete redesign of an online learning platform that increased course completion rates by 60% and transformed the student learning experience.

Role

UX/UI Designer

Duration

7 months

Team

14 members

Platform

Web & Mobile

The Challenge

EduFlow's existing platform had extremely low course completion rates and poor student engagement. Students were dropping out due to a confusing interface, lack of motivation, and inability to track their progress effectively.

28% Course Completion Rate
15min Avg. Session Duration
2.9 Student Satisfaction

Research & Discovery

Conducted extensive research with students, instructors, and learning experts to understand the complexities of online education.

Student Interviews

Interviewed 40+ students to understand their learning habits, motivations, and barriers to completion.

Learning Analytics

Analyzed learning patterns and drop-off points to identify critical moments in the student journey.

Instructor Feedback

Gathered insights from educators about what makes online courses engaging and effective.

Cognitive Load Testing

Tested different interface designs to measure cognitive load and learning effectiveness.

Key Research Findings

  • Students need clear progress tracking and milestone celebrations to stay motivated
  • Bite-sized learning sessions work better than long video lectures
  • Social features and peer interaction significantly improve engagement
  • Students learn better when they can control pacing and replay content
  • Mobile learning is crucial for busy working professionals

Design Solution

Created an engaging, motivating learning experience that adapts to different learning styles and keeps students progressing toward their goals.

Progress Gamification

Achievement system with badges, streaks, and milestones that motivate continued learning.

Adaptive Learning Path

Personalized course progression that adapts to individual learning pace and knowledge gaps.

Social Learning

Discussion forums, study groups, and peer review features that create learning communities.

Mobile-First Experience

Seamless mobile learning with offline capabilities and bite-sized content consumption.

User Experience Design

Course Dashboard

Clean, motivating dashboard showing progress, upcoming lessons, and achievements to encourage continued learning.

Interactive Lessons

Engaging lesson format with video, quizzes, and interactive elements designed to maximize retention and understanding.

Progress Tracking

Comprehensive progress visualization with detailed analytics to help students understand their learning journey.

Design Principles & Insights

  • Motivation is as important as content: Psychological factors like progress visualization and achievements significantly impact learning outcomes.
  • Micro-learning works: Breaking content into smaller, digestible chunks improved both completion rates and retention.
  • Social features drive engagement: Peer interaction and community features kept students motivated and accountable.
  • Mobile accessibility is essential: Many working professionals rely on mobile learning during commutes and breaks.
  • Personalization enhances learning: Adaptive content paths based on learning styles and pace improved effectiveness.
  • Clear feedback loops are crucial: Students need immediate feedback on their progress and performance to stay engaged.
