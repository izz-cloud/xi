E-commerce Mobile App Redesign

Complete redesign of a mobile e-commerce app that resulted in 40% increased user engagement and 25% higher conversion rates.

Role

Lead Product Designer

Duration

4 months

Team

8 members

Platform

iOS & Android

The Challenge

ShopEasy, a growing e-commerce platform, was experiencing declining user engagement and conversion rates on their mobile app. Users were abandoning their shopping carts, and the overall user experience felt outdated and confusing.

68% Cart Abandonment Rate
2.3% Conversion Rate
3.2 App Store Rating

Research & Discovery

I conducted comprehensive user research to understand the pain points and identify opportunities for improvement.

User Interviews

Conducted 20 in-depth interviews with current and potential users to understand their shopping behaviors and frustrations.

Analytics Review

Analyzed user behavior data to identify drop-off points and areas of friction in the current user flow.

Competitive Analysis

Evaluated competing e-commerce apps to identify best practices and differentiation opportunities.

Usability Testing

Tested the existing app with 15 users to identify specific usability issues and pain points.

Key Findings

  • Complex navigation made it difficult for users to find products
  • Checkout process was too long with unnecessary steps
  • Product images were low quality and didn't showcase items well
  • Search functionality was limited and didn't understand user intent
  • No clear way to save items for later or create wishlists

The Solution

Based on research insights, I designed a streamlined, user-friendly experience focused on reducing friction and increasing conversion.

Simplified Navigation

Redesigned the information architecture with a clear category structure and improved search functionality.

Enhanced Product Pages

High-quality image galleries, detailed descriptions, and user reviews to build trust and confidence.

Streamlined Checkout

Reduced checkout from 6 steps to 3, with guest checkout option and multiple payment methods.

Personalization

AI-driven product recommendations and personalized shopping experience based on user behavior.

Design Process

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity wireframes to explore different layout options and user flows. Focused on information hierarchy and content organization.

High-Fidelity Designs

Developed detailed visual designs with a modern, clean aesthetic that aligns with the brand while prioritizing usability.

Prototyping

Built interactive prototypes to test user flows and validate design decisions before development.

Results

The redesigned app launched successfully with significant improvements across all key metrics:

+40%
User Engagement
+25%
Conversion Rate
-32%
Cart Abandonment
4.6
App Store Rating

User Feedback

"The new app is so much easier to use! I can find what I'm looking for quickly and the checkout process is smooth. It actually makes me want to shop more." - Sarah, App User

Lessons Learned

  • User research is crucial: The insights from user interviews directly informed our design decisions and helped us prioritize features.
  • Simplicity wins: Reducing complexity in navigation and checkout had the biggest impact on user satisfaction.
  • Test early and often: Regular usability testing throughout the process helped us catch and fix issues before launch.
  • Collaborate closely: Working closely with developers ensured our designs were feasible and launched smoothly.
