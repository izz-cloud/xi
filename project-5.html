Food Delivery App

Complete redesign of a food delivery app that resulted in 50% increase in orders and significantly improved user satisfaction.

Role

Product Designer

Duration

4 months

Team

10 members

Platform

iOS & Android

The Problem

QuickEats was struggling with low conversion rates and poor user retention. The existing app was confusing to navigate, had a lengthy ordering process, and lacked personalization features that users expected from modern food delivery apps.

3.2% Conversion Rate
23% User Retention
4.7min Avg. Order Time

Understanding Users

Conducted comprehensive research to understand user behaviors, preferences, and pain points in the food ordering experience.

User Journey Mapping

Mapped the complete food ordering journey from craving to delivery to identify friction points.

Behavioral Analysis

Analyzed user behavior data to understand drop-off points and popular features.

Contextual Interviews

Interviewed users in their natural environment during meal times to understand real needs.

A/B Testing

Tested different interface concepts to validate design decisions with real user behavior.

User Insights

  • Users want to order food quickly, especially during busy meal times
  • Visual appeal of food photos significantly impacts ordering decisions
  • Personalized recommendations increase order value and satisfaction
  • Real-time order tracking reduces anxiety and improves trust
  • Group ordering features are highly valued for office and family orders

Design Strategy

Created a streamlined, visually appealing experience focused on speed, personalization, and delight throughout the ordering journey.

Quick Reordering

One-tap reordering system that learns from user preferences and frequently ordered items.

Visual Discovery

High-quality food photography and visual browsing that makes food selection appetizing and easy.

Smart Recommendations

AI-powered suggestions based on time of day, weather, previous orders, and user preferences.

Seamless Checkout

Simplified payment flow with saved preferences and multiple payment options for quick completion.

Design Process

Restaurant Discovery

Intuitive browsing experience with smart filters, cuisine categories, and personalized restaurant recommendations.

Menu & Ordering

Clear menu presentation with appetizing photos, detailed descriptions, and easy customization options.

Order Tracking

Real-time order tracking with estimated delivery times and driver location for transparency and peace of mind.

Results & Business Impact

The redesigned app delivered significant improvements across all key metrics:

+50%
Order Volume
+85%
User Retention
-65%
Order Time
+32%
Average Order Value

Business Outcomes

  • Monthly active users increased by 78% within 6 months of launch
  • Customer satisfaction scores improved from 3.1 to 4.6 stars
  • Reduced customer support queries by 45% due to clearer UX
  • Increased restaurant partner satisfaction due to higher order volumes
  • App store rating improved from 2.8 to 4.5 stars
"The new app is so much better! I can find what I want quickly, and the recommendations are spot-on. Ordering food has never been this easy and enjoyable." - Jessica, Regular User

Key Takeaways

  • Speed is everything in food delivery: Every second counts when users are hungry and in a hurry.
  • Visual design drives appetite: High-quality food photography directly correlates with order conversion.
  • Personalization increases loyalty: Users appreciate when the app learns their preferences and suggests relevant options.
  • Transparency builds trust: Clear order tracking and delivery estimates reduce user anxiety significantly.
  • Mobile-first is critical: The majority of food orders happen on mobile devices during specific time windows.
