Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope, but most projects range from 2-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with small startups?

Absolutely! I love working with startups and understand the unique challenges of early-stage companies. I offer flexible packages to fit different budgets.

What's included in your design process?

My process includes research, strategy, wireframing, visual design, prototyping, and developer handoff. I'll customize the approach based on your needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I offer post-launch support and can help with design system maintenance, user testing, and iterative improvements.

How do you handle revisions?

I include multiple revision rounds in my packages and work collaboratively throughout the process to ensure the final design meets your expectations.

Can you work with remote teams?

Definitely! I'm experienced working with distributed teams and use tools like Figma, Slack, and Zoom to collaborate effectively.
